# 1. The Correct Tone for Your Product

The ideal tone is:

**Simple + Practical + Honest**

Not clever.
Not fancy.
Not technical.

The user should feel:

> “This is built by someone who understands how institutes actually work.”

### Tone characteristics

* Clear
* Practical
* Honest
* Slightly conversational
* Operational (focused on daily work)

Example tone:

> Capture every enquiry.
> Follow up on time.
> Convert more students.

Very simple. Very direct.

# 2. Tone You Must Avoid

These tones **hurt adoption** for your audience.

### Startup marketing tone

Example:

> A powerful admission operating system designed to revolutionize your institute operations.

This sounds **fake to institute owners**.

---

### Enterprise SaaS tone

Example:

> Advanced pipeline management with role-based operational control.

This sounds **complex and intimidating**.

---

### Overhyped tone

Example:

> Transform your admission ecosystem with next-generation automation.

This destroys trust.

---

# 3. Practical Tone Rules

Use these **five rules everywhere**.

### Rule 1: Speak like a human, not software

Bad:

> Manage your structured lead pipeline.

Good:

> Track all your enquiries in one place.

---

### Rule 2: Use everyday words

Use words they already use:

* enquiry
* follow-up
* admission
* student
* course

Avoid:

* lead pipeline
* CRM
* workflow
* automation system

---

### Rule 3: Focus on problems they already feel

They already experience:

* missed calls
* forgotten follow-ups
* messy notebooks
* WhatsApp overload

Speak to those.

Example:

> Never forget a follow-up again.

---

### Rule 4: Avoid exaggeration

Be honest.

Bad:

> The most advanced admission system ever built.

Good:

> A simple system to manage admission enquiries.

---

### Rule 5: Use short sentences

Example:

Bad:

> Our platform enables streamlined admission operations for educational institutions.

Good:

> Manage all admission enquiries in one place.

---

# 4. Tone Examples for Your Product

Here are **tone-correct versions** of your product language.

---

### Homepage headline

Good:

> Never miss an admission enquiry again.

Subtext:

> Capture enquiries, track follow-ups, and manage admissions in one simple system.

---

### Problem statement

> Many coaching institutes track enquiries on Excel, WhatsApp, or notebooks.
> Follow-ups get missed and some students never get called back.

---

### Solution statement

> Classes360 keeps all your enquiries in one place and reminds you when to follow up.

---

### Feature language

Instead of:

> Structured Lead Pipeline

Use:

> See all enquiries in one place.

---

Instead of:

> Role-based access control

Use:

> Your admission staff can manage their own enquiries.

---

Instead of:

> Student records with course mapping

> Track which students joined which courses.

---

### Pricing tone

Good:

> One admission pays for the system.

---

### Trust tone

> No long-term contract.
> Cancel anytime.
> Your institute owns its data.
