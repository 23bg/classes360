# Classes360 Notification Event System

This document defines the application-event based notification contract for Classes360.

## Channels

- IN_APP_ADMIN
- IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
- WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS
- WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- EMAIL

## Audiences

- INSTITUTE_OWNER
- INSTITUTE_TEAM_MEMBER
- INSTITUTE_USERS
- STUDENT

Notes:
- INSTITUTE_USERS includes owner + team members.
- INSTITUTE_TEAM_MEMBER means assigned counsellor or staff member.

## WhatsApp Sender Types

- ONCAMPUS_SYSTEM_NUMBER
- INSTITUTE_WHATSAPP_NUMBER

Rules:
- ONCAMPUS_SYSTEM_NUMBER is used for lead alerts, follow-up reminders, and subscription alerts.
- INSTITUTE_WHATSAPP_NUMBER is used for student communication, fee alerts, and announcements.
- If institute sender is not connected, routing falls back to Classes360 system sender.

## Event Matrix

### Public Enquiry

- PUBLIC_ENQUIRY_SUBMITTED
  - channels: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS
  - recipient: INSTITUTE_USERS
  - whatsapp sender: ONCAMPUS_SYSTEM_NUMBER
- PUBLIC_ENQUIRY_CONFIRMATION
  - channels: WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE, EMAIL
  - recipient: STUDENT
  - whatsapp sender: INSTITUTE_WHATSAPP_NUMBER

### Lead / Enquiry Workflow

- LEAD_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS -> INSTITUTE_USERS
- LEAD_ASSIGNED: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS -> INSTITUTE_TEAM_MEMBER
- LEAD_STATUS_CHANGED: IN_APP_ADMIN -> INSTITUTE_USERS
- LEAD_NOTE_ADDED: IN_APP_ADMIN -> INSTITUTE_USERS
- FOLLOW_UP_SCHEDULED: IN_APP_ADMIN -> INSTITUTE_TEAM_MEMBER
- FOLLOW_UP_COMPLETED: IN_APP_ADMIN -> INSTITUTE_USERS
- FOLLOW_UP_REMINDER: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS -> INSTITUTE_TEAM_MEMBER
- LEAD_CONVERTED_TO_STUDENT: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS -> INSTITUTE_OWNER

WhatsApp sender: ONCAMPUS_SYSTEM_NUMBER

### Student Lifecycle

- STUDENT_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- STUDENT_UPDATED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- STUDENT_BATCH_CHANGED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- STUDENT_COURSE_CHANGED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- STUDENT_PORTAL_CREDENTIALS_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE, EMAIL

WhatsApp sender: INSTITUTE_WHATSAPP_NUMBER

### Course and Batch

- COURSE_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- COURSE_UPDATED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
- BATCH_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- BATCH_UPDATED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- TEACHER_ASSIGNED_TO_BATCH: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL

### Fee and Payment

- FEE_PLAN_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
- INSTALLMENT_ADDED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
- PAYMENT_RECEIVED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE, EMAIL
- PAYMENT_FAILED: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE, EMAIL
- FEE_DUE_REMINDER: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE, EMAIL

WhatsApp sender: INSTITUTE_WHATSAPP_NUMBER

### Announcements

- ANNOUNCEMENT_CREATED
  - channels: IN_APP_ADMIN, IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE, EMAIL
  - recipient: STUDENT
  - whatsapp sender: INSTITUTE_WHATSAPP_NUMBER

### Team Management

- TEAM_MEMBER_ADDED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- TEAM_MEMBER_ROLE_UPDATED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- TEAM_MEMBER_REMOVED: IN_APP_ADMIN

### Institute

- INSTITUTE_PROFILE_UPDATED: IN_APP_ADMIN
- INSTITUTE_ONBOARDING_COMPLETED: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS

WhatsApp sender: ONCAMPUS_SYSTEM_NUMBER

### Subscription and Billing

- TRIAL_STARTED: IN_APP_ADMIN, EMAIL
- TRIAL_ENDING_SOON: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS, EMAIL
- SUBSCRIPTION_CREATED: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS, EMAIL
- SUBSCRIPTION_PAYMENT_SUCCESS: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS, EMAIL
- SUBSCRIPTION_PAYMENT_FAILED: IN_APP_ADMIN, WHATSAPP_ONCAMPUS, EMAIL
- SUBSCRIPTION_CANCELLED: IN_APP_ADMIN, EMAIL

Recipient: INSTITUTE_OWNER
WhatsApp sender: ONCAMPUS_SYSTEM_NUMBER

### Student Portal Activity

- PORTAL_LOGIN: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
- PORTAL_PASSWORD_CHANGED: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
- NEW_ANNOUNCEMENT_AVAILABLE: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- NEW_PAYMENT_RECORDED: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- FEE_DUE_SOON: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- BATCH_UPDATED: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL, WHATSAPP_INSTITUTE
- COURSE_UPDATED: IN_APP_STUDENT_PORTAL
